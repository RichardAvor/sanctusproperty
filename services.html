Our Services

Tailored Property Sourcing

Quite simply, we work with all our clients on a one-to-one basis. We will source below market value (BMV) properties for you specifically, based on your requirements and specifications. We believe that everyone’s property goals are different and one size certainly does not fit all, therefore, we will only recommend properties to our clients that fits their needs.

We advise and source properties based on a few key principles:

Location
We aim to focus on areas with favourable demand and supply dynamics, potential for high rental yields and/or capital growth
Tenant Profile
If you are looking for a buy to let investment, what types of tenants would you like to rent your property to?
Value Add
Properties with opportunities for refurbishments that can increase the overall value
Exit Strategy
Before we source any properties for you, we will work with you to understand and develop an exit strategy. Are you looking for buy-to -flip or buy-to-hold investments?

We do not take any sourcing fees directly until property contracts have been exchanged. Our work is based on transparency and honesty which means we will keep you involved in the process as much as you wish to be.

Vendors

If you are looking to sell your property but do not want go through the hassle of dealing directly with estate agents, we are able to work with you to find the right buyer for your property. Property sales’ processes can be long winded and complicated; we aim to make it as smooth a transition as possible.

Contact us to discuss your options and the potential to sell your property through us.

Property Portfolio Management

Are you looking to build a portfolio of properties but you lack experience and/or time to do this effectively? We have in-house skills and capabilities you can leverage to target acquisition opportunities that align with your investment strategy. We offer investment advice, oversight on your investments and source multiple properties for your portfolio, allowing you to focus on other areas of your life.

We are committed to driving forward your investment plans, achieving maximum rental yields and capital growth through redevelopment opportunities in highly sought after locations.

Contact us to find out more about our tailored property portfolio management services.
