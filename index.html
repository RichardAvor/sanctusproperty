What we Do

Sanctus Property Group is a property investment company; we offer a guaranteed rental scheme for landlords. We can take on your property on a long term lease and you receive monthly payments whether the property is 'let' or 'vacant'.

We specialise in offering tailored property solutions to all our clients.

We work with vendors across London and the South East looking to sell or rent their properties at good values, to meet their financial needs.

Are you looking to build a property portfolio? We are able to help you start, build and maintain a portfolio of suitable properties. Whether you are looking for vanilla buy-to-lets or properties with development opportunities, we will provide comprehensive advice and work with you every step of the way. Our goal is to enrich the lives of our clients through sound property investment decisions.

3-Wins

Our philosophy is that everyone involved in a property exchange should be a winner; the tenant (buyer), the landlord (seller) and the middleman (us). No one should be left worse off as a result of engaging in a deal; this drives the quality of our services, our integrity and the way we do business.
